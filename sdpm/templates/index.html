What's My Own Tale about?

My Own Tale is a original way of creating your own RPG browser games.
The main concept is to allow RPG players and Masters to get along and have fun,
so we try at best to keep RPG Table game.

The main difference between other RPG Browser games is that you'll have a map, and all the time you are interactin with it, even when you are
buying a potion or attacking a monster, no more: "Click this link and go to town, kill the mosters, go back and read the report".

Also it has some very easy RPG creation interface, for those who prefer to create the adventures but don't understand much of programming to do it.
But, even with the user-friendly interface, if you want to add more elaborated stuff in the gameplay, you can change the scripts of your RPG, or even create your own!
